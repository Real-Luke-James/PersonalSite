UIN Resolution 1282

Policies defining Suspension from the UIN

This UIN Resolution aims to establish the requirements allowing for UIN Suspension and the effects of UIN Suspension.

Resolution Chapters

  1. If a country member loses membership by any means from the 5JA SMP Companion Discord Server,
    they can be suspended from the UIN until they regain the ability to rejoin.

    1. If the country leader is banned from the 5JA SMP Companion Discord Server,
      their country is considered dissolved, and its land may be divided among other
      UIN member states.

    2. In a case where they voluntarily leave the 5JA SMP Companion Discord Server,
      a UIN member state may host a vote to suspend them from the UIN under this
      section.

    3. In a case where they are banned from the 5J Airlines Discord but not the 5JA
      SMP Companion Discord Server, a UIN member state may host a vote to
      suspend them from the UIN under this section.
